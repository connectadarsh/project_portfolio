Web Design

Web design is about making websites that look nice and are easy to use. It involves arranging things like how the page looks, what colors and fonts to use, and adding pictures to make it all look good. Good web design isn't just about making things pretty; it's also about making sure people can use the website easily. This means understanding how people use websites, making sure it works well on different devices, and that everyone can access it easily.
